Devil's Kiss (cocktail)

A ghoulish cocktail popular in some of the weirder dive bars on the system fringe.

OOC Information

Cocktails will change the name of bartending glasses when mixed properly.

This cocktail is mixed with the following ingredients:
  • 4 parts dark rum
  • 1 part blood
  • 2 parts coffee liqueur
