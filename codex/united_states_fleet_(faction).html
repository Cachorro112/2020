United States Fleet (faction)

The United States Navy is the Space branch of the U.S. Armed Forces, tasked with defending America's interests at space. It boasts a formidable fleet of ships, equipped with advanced weaponry and technology. the Fleet has played pivotal roles in both peacetime operations and major conflicts, projecting American power globally (and extraplanetary). It operates around earth and deep space, conducting missions ranging from patrol to humanitarian assistance and disaster relief. Comprised of dedicated sailors and officers, the Fleet upholds a tradition of excellence, valor, and service to the nation.
