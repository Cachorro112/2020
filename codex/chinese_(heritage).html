Chinese (heritage)

You are from China, the actual regime support the United States on the ongoing tensions between the United States and the Soviet Union. China is officially known as the Republic of China (ROC), existed in a state of tension due to Communist Guerrilas on the Inner Mongolia and Tibet. Despite this, China experienced remarkable economic growth and modernization, transforming from an agrarian society into a high-tech manufacturing powerhouse known as the 'Chinese Miracle'. China also faced the constant threat of military confrontation with the Soviet Union, as well as the challenge of asserting its power on the global stage.
