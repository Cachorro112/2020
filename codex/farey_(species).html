Farey (species)

an omnivorous mammalian creature from the planet cassini IV/Korevak. recently discovered by mankind. They have a biology almost identical to humans, but their genetics are similar to those of terrestrial lupines and foxes.

OOC Information

They have the following notable traits:
  • Whitelist restricted.
  • They breathe oxygen.
  • They exhale carbon dioxide.
  • Carbon dioxide is poisonous to them.
