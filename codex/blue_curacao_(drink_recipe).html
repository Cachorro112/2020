Blue Curacao (drink recipe)

Exotically blue, fruity drink, distilled from oranges.

OOC Information

This recipe produces 10u blue curacao.
It should be performed in a glass or shaker, and requires the following ingredients: 10u orange juice (catalysts: 5u universal enzyme)]
