Ascent Milieu (heritage)

The Ascent is an ancient, isolated stellar empire composed of the mantid-cephalopodean Kharmaani and their gaggle of AI servitors. Day to day existence in the Ascent is largely a matter of navigating a bewildering labyrinth of social obligations, gyne power dynamics, factional tithing, protection rackets, industry taxes and plain old interpersonal backstabbing. Both member cultures of this stellar power are eusocial to an extent, and their society is shaped around the teeming masses of workers, soldiers, technicians and 'lesser' citizens supporting a throng of imperious and all-powerful queens.
