money cannon

These devices were invented several centuries ago and are a distinctly human cultural infection. They have produced knockoffs as timeless and as insipid as the potato gun and the paddle ball, showing up in all corners of the galaxy. The Money Cannon variation is noteworthy for its sturdiness and build quality, but is, at the end of the day, just another knockoff of the ancient originals.

OOC Information

Load money into the cannon by picking it up with the gun, or feeding it directly by hand. Use in your hand to configure how much money you want to fire per shot.

Antagonist Information

Sliding a cryptographic sequencer into the receptacle will short the motors and override their speed. If you set the cannon to dispense 100 units or more, this might make a handy weapon.
