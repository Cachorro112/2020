Belter (heritage)

You are from the Inner Belt These colonies, established by a combination of government initiatives and private enterprises, are pioneering of space exploration and resource exploitation. With the United States, Soviet Union and private mining companies leading the way, the Inner Belt colonies thrive on the extraction of valuable resources such as water ice, iron, and rare metals from asteroids. Despite the challenges of isolation, limited resources, and harsh environmental conditions, the inhabitants of these colonies are driven by the promise of wealth, scientific discovery, and the opportunity to establish a new human presence beyond the confines of Earth.
