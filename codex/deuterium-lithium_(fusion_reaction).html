deuterium-lithium (fusion reaction)

OOC Information

Fusion between deuterium and lithium can be achieved with a plasma temperature of 373.15 Kelvin or higher.
This reaction consumes 4 heat units and produces 0 heat units.
The process has an instability rating of 2 and a radiation rating of 6.
In the process of deuterium-lithium fusion, tritium is produced.
