Sign Language (language)

A sign language commonly used for those who are deaf or mute.
Shorthand: 'HS'

OOC Information

Key to use it: ',s'
It has a significant non-verbal component. Speech is garbled without line-of-sight.
It is completely non-verbal, using gestures or signs to communicate.
It will not be affected by speech impediments.
