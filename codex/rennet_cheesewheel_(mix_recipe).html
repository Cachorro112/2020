Rennet Cheesewheel (mix recipe)

A big wheel of delcious cheese.

OOC Information

This recipe produces a wheel.
It should be performed in a mixing bowl or beaker, and requires the following ingredients: 40u milk + 3u rennet
The recipe will not succeed if the temperature is above 413.15K.
The recipe will not succeed if the temperature is below 313.15K.
