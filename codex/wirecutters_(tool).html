wirecutters (tool)

With modern alloys, today's wirecutters can snap through cables of astonishing thickness.

OOC Information

This tool will cut wiring anywhere you see it - make sure to wear insulated gloves! When used on more complicated machines or airlocks, it can not only cut cables, but repair them, as well.

Antagonist Information

These cutters can be used to cripple the power anywhere on the ship. All it takes is some creativity, and being in the right place at the right time.
