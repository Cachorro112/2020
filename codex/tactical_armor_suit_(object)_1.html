tactical armor suit

An advanced multi-plated composite vest with kevlar lining and plenty of room to move. 'Delta-8' is sewn into the left pauldron, and 'Morning Sun' is sewn into the right.

OOC Information

It provides good protection even against more penetrating melee weapons.
It is rated to protect against rifle rounds.
It is rated to protect against the beams of laser handguns.
It provides almost complete shielding against thermal and electrical energy.
It provides major padding and insulation against explosions.
It provides almost complete protection against biohazards
It provides strong shielding against radiation.
It is airtight.
This suit is rated for pressures up to 10132.5 kPa.
The material is exceptionally thick.
You could probably safely skydive into the Sun wearing this.
It provides very good protection against very cold temperatures.
It covers the upper body, lower body, legs, feet, arms, and hands.
It can be worn on your exo slot.
