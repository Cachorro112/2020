exotic matter (substance)

Hypercrystalline supermatter is a subset of non-baryonic 'exotic' matter. It is found mostly in the heart of large stars, and features heavily in all kinds of fringe physics-defying technology.

OOC Information

This substance has the following properties in standard temperature and pressure:
    • Its melting point is 1000 K.
    • Its boiling point is 30000 K.
    • It is radioactive.
    • It can be used in a fusion reaction.
    As a gas or vapor, it has the following properties:
    • It has a specific heat of 20 J/(mol*K).
    • It has a molar mass of 0.232 kg/mol.
    • It can be used as fuel in a fusion reaction.
    As a building or crafting material, it has the following properties:
    • It has average resistance to physical impacts.
    • It has average resistance to applied energy.
    • It conducts electricity.
    • It is of average weight.
    • It is ~1.7 times softer than steel.
    • It is as durable as steel.
