Enzyme Margarine (mix recipe)

A stick of emulsified plant oil, often used as a substitute for butter.

OOC Information

This recipe produces a stick.
It should be performed in a mixing bowl or beaker, and requires the following ingredients: 1u sodium chloride + 20u plant oil (catalysts: 5u universal enzyme)]
The recipe will not succeed if the temperature is above 413.15K.
The recipe will not succeed if the temperature is below 313.15K.
