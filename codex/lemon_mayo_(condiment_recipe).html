Lemon Mayo (condiment recipe)

A mixture of egg yolk with lemon juice or vinegar. Usually put on bland food to make it more edible.

OOC Information

This recipe produces 10u mayonnaise.
It should be performed in a mixing bowl or beaker, and requires the following ingredients: 5u lemon juice + 5u egg yolk
