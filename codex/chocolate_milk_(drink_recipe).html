Chocolate Milk (drink recipe)

A mixture of perfectly healthy milk and delicious chocolate.

OOC Information

This recipe produces 5u chocolate milk.
It should be performed in a glass or shaker, and requires the following ingredients: 5u milk + 1u coco powder
The recipe will not succeed if the temperature is above 343.15K.
