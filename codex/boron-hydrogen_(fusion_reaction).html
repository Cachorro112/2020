boron-hydrogen (fusion reaction)

OOC Information

Fusion between boron and hydrogen can be achieved with a plasma temperature of 373.15 Kelvin or higher.
This reaction consumes 6 heat units and produces 30 heat units.
The process has an instability rating of 6 and a radiation rating of 6.
Nothing is produced in the process of boron-hydrogen fusion.
