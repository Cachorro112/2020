Mixology 101

Here's a guide for mixing decent cocktails.

Grog

Mix 1 part water and 1 part dark rum in a glass.

Screwdriver

Mix 4 parts orange juice and 1 part vodka in a glass.

Tequila sunrise

Mix 4 parts orange juice and 1 part tequila in a glass.

Gin martini

Mix 4 parts gin and 1 part vermouth in a glass.

Vodka martini

Mix 4 parts vodka and 1 part vermouth in a glass.

Allies Cocktail

Mix 2 parts vermouth, 2 parts vodka, and 2 parts gin in a glass.

Bilk

Mix 1 part beer and 1 part milk in a glass.

Gin and tonic

Mix 4 parts tonic water and 1 part gin in a glass.

Cuba Libre

Mix 4 parts cola and 1 part dark rum in a glass.

Black Russian

Mix 2 parts vodka and 1 part coffee liqueur in a glass.

White Russian

Mix 2 parts coffee liqueur, cream to taste, and 1 part vodka in a glass.

Whiskey cola

Mix 4 parts cola and 1 part malt whiskey in a glass.

Bloody Mary

Mix 3 parts tomato juice, 1 part vodka, and 1 part lime juice in a glass.

The Livergeist

Mix 1 part vodka, 1 part gin, 1 part aged whiskey, 1 part cognac, and 1 part lime juice in a glass.

Brave Bull

Mix 2 parts tequila and 1 part coffee liqueur in a glass.

Toxins Special

Mix 1 part dark rum, 1 part vermouth, and phoron to taste in a glass.

Beepsky Smash

Mix 2 parts malt whiskey, 1 part lime juice, and iron to taste in a glass.

Doctor's Delight

Mix 3 parts tricordrazine, 1 part lime juice, 1 part tomato juice, 1 part orange juice, and cream to taste in a glass.

The Manly Dorf

Mix 1 part ale and 1 part beer in a glass.

Irish coffee

Mix 4 parts coffee and 1 part Irish cream in a glass.

B-52

Mix 1 part coffee liqueur, 1 part Irish cream, and 1 part cognac in a glass.

Atomic Bomb

Mix 1 part coffee liqueur, 1 part Irish cream, 1 part cognac, and uranium to taste in a glass.

Margarita

Mix 3 parts tequila and 1 part lime juice in a glass.

Long Island Iced Tea

Mix 2 parts cola, 1 part dark rum, 1 part vodka, 1 part gin, and 1 part tequila in a glass.

Three Mile Island Iced Tea

Mix 2 parts cola, 1 part dark rum, 1 part vodka, 1 part gin, 1 part tequila, and uranium to taste in a glass.

Whiskey soda

Mix 4 parts soda water and 1 part malt whiskey in a glass.

Manhattan

Mix 2 parts malt whiskey and 1 part vermouth in a glass.

Manhattan Project

Mix 2 parts malt whiskey, 1 part vermouth, and uranium to taste in a glass.

Vodka and tonic

Mix 4 parts tonic water and 1 part vodka in a glass.

Gin fizz

Mix 2 parts gin, 2 parts soda water, and 1 part lime juice in a glass.

Bahama Mama

Mix 2 parts dark rum, 2 parts orange juice, 2 parts lime juice, and 1 part grenadine syrup in a glass.

Singulo

Mix 1 part vodka, 1 part red wine, and radium to taste in a glass.

Demon's Blood

Mix 2 parts dark rum, 2 parts citrus soda, 2 parts cherry soda, and 1 part blood in a glass.

Booger

Mix 2 parts cream, 2 parts dark rum, 1 part banana juice, and 1 part watermelon juice in a glass.

Anti-freeze

Mix 3 parts vodka, 2 parts cream, and 2 parts ice in a glass.

Barefoot

Mix 4 parts vermouth, 2 parts berry juice, and 1 part cream in a glass.

Sbiten

Mix 9 parts mead and 1 part capsaicin oil in a glass.

Red mead

Mix 1 part mead and 1 part blood in a glass.

Acid Spit

Mix 1 part red wine and sulfuric acid to taste in a glass.

Changeling Sting

Mix 2 parts orange juice, 1 part lime juice, 1 part lemon juice, and 1 part vodka in a glass.

Neurotoxin

Mix 1 part vodka, 1 part gin, 1 part aged whiskey, 1 part cognac, 1 part lime juice, and midazolam to taste in a glass.

Snow White

Mix 3 parts lemon-lime soda and 1 part beer in a glass.

Irish Slammer

Mix 5 parts ale, 1 part malt whiskey, and 1 part Irish cream in a glass.

Syndicate Bomb

Mix 1 part malt whiskey, 1 part beer, and 4 parts cola in a glass.

Devil's Kiss

Mix 4 parts dark rum, 1 part blood, and 2 parts coffee liqueur in a glass.

Hippy's Delight

Mix 1 part vodka, 1 part gin, 1 part aged whiskey, 1 part cognac, 1 part lime juice, and 2 parts psychotropics in a glass.

Banana Honk

Mix 4 parts banana juice, 2 parts cream, and 1 part sugar in a glass.

Ship's Surgeon

Mix 4 parts cherry soda and 2 parts dark rum in a glass.

Vodka cola

Mix 2 parts cola and 1 part vodka in a glass.

Sawbones' Dismay

Mix 1 part Jagermeister and 1 part Beast Energy in a glass.

Patron

Mix 1 part tequila and silver to taste in a glass.

Rewriter

Mix 1 part coffee and 1 part citrus soda in a glass.

Goldschlager

Mix 1 part vodka and gold to taste in a glass.

Brown Dwarf

Mix 2 parts hot chocolate and 1 part citrus soda in a glass.

Snowball

Mix 3 parts ice, 2 parts coffee, and 1 part watermelon juice in a glass.

Fool's Gold

Mix 1 part water and 1 part malt whiskey in a glass.
