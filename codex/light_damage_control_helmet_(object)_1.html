light damage control helmet

A lighter polymer helmet commonly used by engineers and first responders throughout the human space. It comes with a built-in flashlight and has 'Damage Control' written on its back in yellow lettering.

OOC Information

It will likely blunt even large blades like swords, and cushion impacts from heavy weapons.
It provides close to no protection against bullets.
It provides some shielding against thermal and electrical energy.
It provides padding and insulation against explosions.
It provides minor protection against biohazards
This suit is rated for pressures up to 10132.5 kPa.
You could probably safely skydive into the Sun wearing this.
It covers the head.
It can be worn on your head.
