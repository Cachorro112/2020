Literacy (skill)

Your ability to read and write.

OOC Information

Unskilled

You are completely incapable of reading or writing.

Basic

With time, effort and patience, you are able to read and write at a very simple level.

Trained

You can read and write fluently, without delays or too many misunderstandings.

Experienced

You can quickly and easily digest complex information, and spend less time for a higher benefit from reading textbooks.

Master

Your mastery of the written word is such that you are able to produce your own textbooks for others to use. You can write a textbook about any skill you have personally trained in.
