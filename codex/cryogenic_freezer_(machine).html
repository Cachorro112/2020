cryogenic freezer (machine)

OOC Information

To enter a cryopod, or put someone in a cryopod, click+drag. When someone is in a cryopod, and they use the ghost verb, their character will despawn, be removed from the game round along with all their belongings, and freeing their job slot for someone else to take. This also occurs if a character is inside a cryopod for 15 minutes. Any particularly important belongings that the game can't do without will be stored. The item can then be retrieved from the nearby oversight console.
