Worldnet (language)

The mantid aliens of the Ascent maintain an extensive self-supporting broadcast network for use in team communications.
Shorthand: 'KB'

OOC Information

Key to use it: ',['
It has a significant non-verbal component. Speech is garbled without line-of-sight.
It's a 'hivemind' language, broadcast to all creatures who understand it.
It will not be affected by speech impediments.
