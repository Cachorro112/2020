toolbox

No one remembers which company designed this particular toolbox. It's been mass-produced, retired, brought out of retirement, and counterfeited for decades.

OOC Information

The toolbox is a general-purpose storage item with lots of space. With an item in your hand, click on it to store it inside.

Antagonist Information

Carrying one of these and being bald tends to instill a certain primal fear in most people.
