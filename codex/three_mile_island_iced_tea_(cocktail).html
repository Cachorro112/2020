Three Mile Island Iced Tea (cocktail)

Much like the Atomic Bomb, this cocktail was adapted by asteroid miners who couldn't enjoy a drink without a dose of radiation poisoning.

OOC Information

Cocktails will change the name of bartending glasses when mixed properly.

This cocktail is mixed with the following ingredients:
  • 2 parts cola
  • 1 part dark rum
  • 1 part vodka
  • 1 part gin
  • 1 part tequila
  • uranium to taste
