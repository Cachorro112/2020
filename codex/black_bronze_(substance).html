black bronze (substance)

An alloy of copper and silver. Used in ancient ceremonial gear.

OOC Information


This substance can be produced by:
  • Alloying 2u copper and 1u silver, producing 3u black bronze.

This substance has the following properties in standard temperature and pressure:
    • Its melting point is 1000 K.
    • Its boiling point is 30000 K.
    As a gas or vapor, it has the following properties:
    • It has a specific heat of 20 J/(mol*K).
    • It has a molar mass of 0.232 kg/mol.
    As a building or crafting material, it has the following properties:
    • It is resistant to physical impacts.
    • It is resistant to applied energy.
    • It conducts electricity.
    • It is very heavy.
    • It is as hard as steel.
    • It is as durable as steel.
