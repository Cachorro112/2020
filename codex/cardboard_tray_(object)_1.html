cardboard tray

A cardboard tray to serve food on.

A simple tool allowing for multiple items to be carried at once while keeping the load more accessible than a box or bag. Used primarily by hospitality workers and other service staff.

OOC Information

Storage items are holdable or wearable objects used for storing items. You can hold an appropriate item in your hand and click on the storage item to put the item inside. You can then take items out by accessing the storage UI. To do this, either hold the storage in your hand and click it with the other, or click+drag the storage item onto yourself (this works if the storage item is not being held too!).

* It can hold small sized items, and tiny sized items.
* It has a capacity of 14. (That means it could potentially hold that many tiny items, or half that many small items.)
* You can hold this storage item in your hand and click on valid items with it to put them into the storage item.
* It can use the 'toggle-mode' object verb which allows gathering all valid objects at a location by clicking on the turf under them.
* It cannot be worn on your body. Please don't try.

Trays, when put down on a tables, drop all their contents onto the table. If you drop the tray in any other way or hit someone with it, all the items it holds will fall off and scatter. You can also examine a tray to see the contents.
