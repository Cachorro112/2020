crowbar (tool)

As is the case with most standard-issue tools, crowbars are a simple and timeless design, the only difference being that advanced materials like plasteel have made them uncommonly tough.

OOC Information

Crowbars have countless uses: click on floor tiles to pry them loose. Use alongside a screwdriver to install or remove windows. Force open emergency shutters, or depowered airlocks. Open the panel of an unlocked APC. Pry a computer's circuit board free. And much more!

Antagonist Information

Need to bypass a bolted door? You can use a crowbar to pry the electronics out of an airlock, provided that it has no power and has been welded shut.
