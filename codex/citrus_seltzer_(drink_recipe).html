Citrus Seltzer (drink recipe)

A tasty blend of fizz and citrus.

OOC Information

This recipe produces 3u citrus seltzer.
It should be performed in a glass or shaker, and requires the following ingredients: 1u orange juice + 1u lime juice + 1u soda water
