frontier mining voidsuit

A cheap prospecting voidsuit. What it lacks in comfort it makes up for in armor plating and street cred.

OOC Information

It will likely blunt even large blades like swords, and cushion impacts from heavy weapons.
It provides close to no protection against bullets.
It provides close to no protection against laser beams.
It provides major padding and insulation against explosions.
It provides almost complete protection against biohazards
It provides minor shielding against radiation.
Wearing this will protect you from the vacuum of space.
This suit is rated for pressures up to 5066.25 kPa.
The material is exceptionally thick.
It provides good protection against fire and heat.
It provides very good protection against very cold temperatures.
It covers the upper body, lower body, tail, legs, feet, arms, and hands.
It can be worn on your exo slot.
